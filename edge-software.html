Instant Replay made easy

Instant replay to your iPad in seconds

How It Works

Connect

existing HD cameras to system.

The EDGE system will connect to your existing HD camcorders with an HDMI cable that is provided with the system. You can connect to your end zone camera or your press box camera with our 1 View system or to both with our 2 View system. All you need is a high definition camcorder with an HDMI out port.

Record

plays with EDGE software.

Your HD camcorder will be connected to your laptop with the EDGE capture device. The capture device will record each play onto the laptop where the EDGE Software will organize and store each video clip and get it ready to send out to the connected iPads in the booth or sideline.

The EDGE Software runs on Windows laptops and we recommend latops that have an i5 processor with at least 8GB of RAM.

View

plays on iPad in 2-3 seconds.

After a play is recorded by the capture device and is in the EDGE Software on your laptop, the play then broadcasts to connected iPads in a few seconds. Once the play is available, it will show up in the EDGE iPad App and you will be able to play that clip over and over as long as the system is running.

Upload

video and ODK directly into Hudl.

After your game is done and you turn off the EDGE system, you will be able to access all the recorded clips on your laptop. This feature will allow you to grab all the video files from the laptop and upload them into Hudl Mercury for film review. You will also be able to upload any ODK information that you tagged during the game in Hudl as well.

Software & App Features

Full Control

Slow motion, fast forward and rewind each play

The EDGE App allows the user to have full control over the video playback. Using the speed control meter at the top of the app, you can play each video clip in slow motion and fast forward, both in play and paused modes for simple control. The custom controls of the EDGE App allow forward and rewind with ergonomically positioned thumb buttons over the video player or the play can be scrubbed back and forth using the progress bar feature. Additionally, the play number is positioned in the top left-hand corner so you can quickly see exactly which play you're viewing.

Visual

Color coded drives by ODK. Draw on video to quickly show players needed adjustments

The EDGE App is designed to be very visual in order to help coaches find the plays they want to review fast. Drives are color coded by ODK and all other features were designed with the same intuitive and visual qualities. Draw over the video to visually show your players needed adjustments. You can draw with three colors: blue, red, yellow and can erase the drawings with the undo button. Drawing on the screen is the simplest way to communicate quickly to your players.

Easy & Predictive Play Tagging

Tag ODK, down & distance, hash, YL, play type, net yards and more

EDGE Replay is the only software that has easy & predictive play tagging. With EDGE Tagging, an assistant coach or student can quickly tag each play with down, distance, hash, YL, play type, play result, net yards and more. EDGE Tagging is also predictive, which means it automatically keeps track of the down, distance, YL, hash, score, time of possession and more. It also predicts the most likely play results for every scenario. The play tagging is so quick and easy that is will become part of your standard workflow which will lead to future EDGE Replay features such as live game stats and opponent tendencies.

Multiple Synced Camera Angles

Connect as many EDGE Recording Boxes to the EDGE Network as you want. Each box will automatically connect and sync with the others on the network. Press the record button in the EDGE iPad app and all the boxes will record and stop at the same time. Each angle will then download onto every iPad that is connected to the network.

Smart Features

Quickly sort clips by tagged information. Save clips to device to show in locker room at half.

The EDGE App comes with many built in smart features that save coach's time and allow them to fully use EDGE Instant Replay to find and exploit their opponent's weaknesses. One of those features is the ability to quickly sort through clips based on the tagged information assigned to each play. This allows each coach to tailor the EDGE App to their coaching position and style. Offensive, defensive, and special teams coaches and filter the plays that matter the most to them, which eliminates clutter and gets them viewing the most valuable plays to quickly see what adjustments need to be made. Coaches can also save certain plays to their iPad and bring them into the locker room to show their players during half time. This can be done without being connected to the wireless network because the plays are saved to the device.

Time Saver

Upload video clips and tagged plays directly into hudl.

All the video clips that are recorded during a game are saved to your laptop and can be easily accessed from the EDGE software and put into Hudl Mercury. You can easily tag each play by ODK, down & distance, hash, YL, play type, net yards, and much more. Each play is is color coordinated based on where it's an offensive, defensive, or kicking play so they're easily identifiable. Once your video is on Hudl you can then import any ODK/tagging information that you did in your EDGE App into Hudl as well. This will save you time uploading video from your camera to your computer and also save you time coding the game's ODK info as well.

Automation

The EDGE software does everything for you. Just use the remote to record/stop each play.

The EDGE Instant Replay system comes fully configured and ready to go right when you receive it. We have made it virtually plug and play with all software and networking configurations already done for you. Just connect your camera to the capture device, then connect the capture device to your computer, connect the EDGE router and antenna to the computer, connect the iPads to the EDGE wireless network, and start filming.

Picture in Picture Angle Switching

Switch between angles by using the picture in picture (PIP) screen in the bottom right corner of the video player. The playback of each angle will stay synced as you switch between angles with the PIP.

Hardware

The EDGE recording box stores all of your footage so you don't need a laptop or capture card!

Compact

Weighing in at 6 Oz and measuring 4”x 2.5” x 1.7”, the EDGE Recording Box is the most compact replay box on the market. The box replaces the laptop and/or capture card that other replay systems use. The box is made out of aluminum, is powder coated, and all the ports are labeled for easy setup.

No Power Outlets Needed

EDGE Replay is completely battery powered, so no power outlets are needed. Other replay systems may need power outlets in the end zone and/or sideline.

No Laptop Needed

The EDGE Recording Box eliminates the need for costly laptops and capture cards.

Powerful

View processed clips in 2-5 seconds. 1,000 ft. wireless range. No internet required.

The EDGE Replay system includes all the networking hardware you need to create a private, closed, and powerful Wi-Fi network. Each iPad will connect to the EDGE Wi-Fi network and will be able to receive each play at distances of up to 1,000 ft. away from the wireless router and antenna. Also, you will never data packages or the internet to run the EDGE Replay system, it would work in the middle of nowhere. Thus, you do there is no internet connection required, which makes setting up and operating the EDGE effortless for your filming crew.

Simple

Easy and quick setup with everything you need included.

The EDGE Replay system is extremely simple and easy to set up. Everything you need is included in the package and comes with easy to follow instructions. All the hardware is already configured and is plug and play ready when you get it. There are only a few cables to connect, after that you will be ready to start recording your first plays.

Reliable

Works every time in small and large stadiums.

The EDGE Replay system was designed to have as few parts as possible and to use the best equipment availble to create an extremely reliable system. All hardware components are directly connected to each other, the only wireless component is the Wi-Fi network, which works in both large and small stadiums at distances of up to 1,000 ft.

Portable

Everything packs up in a waterproof hard case.

Every component of the EDGE Replay system packs up into a waterproof Pelican hard case with a custom foam insert to protect every part of the system while you travel. The hard case is 21" x 17" x 9" and weighs 18 lbs.
