Realtime replay in seconds.

EDGE is an instant replay system that allows coaches and athletes to review all recorded plays from your press box and end zone camera systems on their iPads (Generation 2 or newer).

Coaches and players on the sideline, in the press box, or in the stands can view each recorded play in under 5 seconds after it's filmed with up to 10 iPads viewing at a time. The ability to coach and evaluate opponents in real time instantly creates a competitive advantage against any opponent.

Software & App Features

Full Control

Slow motion, fast forward and rewind each play

The EDGE App allows the user to have full control over the video playback. Using the speed control meter at the top of the app, you can play each video clip in slow motion and fast forward, both in play and paused modes for simple control. The custom controls of the EDGE App allow forward and rewind with ergonomically positioned thumb buttons over the video player or the play can be scrubbed back and forth using the progress bar feature. Additionally, the play number is positioned in the top left-hand corner so you can quickly see exactly which play you're viewing.

Visual

Color coded drives by ODK. Draw on video to quickly show players needed adjustments

The EDGE App is designed to be very visual in order to help coaches find the plays they want to review fast. Drives are color coded by ODK and all other features were designed with the same intuitive and visual qualities. Draw over the video to visually show your players needed adjustments. You can draw with three colors: blue, red, yellow and can erase the drawings with the undo button. Drawing on the screen is the simplest way to communicate quickly to your players.

Play Tagging

Tag ODK, down & distance, hash, YL, play type, net yards and more

Add information to your video clips with the EDGE App in real time and save the data to each play as it gets recorded. Default information, such as, ODK, down, distance, hash, yard line, play type, and net yards is already on the app for you to add to a clip. You can also create and add custom tags like positions, players, and formations. Tagging information to each play allows coaches to filter out plays they do not want to see and also to examine their opponent's tendencies.

Smart Features

Quickly sort clips by tagged information. Save clips to device to show in locker room at half.

The EDGE App comes with many built in smart features that save coach's time and allow them to fully use EDGE Instant Replay to find and exploit their opponent's weaknesses. One of those features is the ability to quickly sort through clips based on the tagged information assigned to each play. This allows each coach to tailor the EDGE App to their coaching position and style. Offensive, defensive, and special teams coaches and filter the plays that matter the most to them, which eliminates clutter and gets them viewing the most valuable plays to quickly see what adjustments need to be made. Coaches can also save certain plays to their iPad and bring them into the locker room to show their players during half time. This can be done without being connected to the wireless network because the plays are saved to the device.

Time Saver

Upload video clips and tagged plays directly into hudl.

All the video clips that are recorded during a game are saved to your laptop and can be easily accessed from the EDGE software and put into Hudl Mercury. You can easily tag each play by ODK, down & distance, hash, YL, play type, net yards, and much more. Each play is is color coordinated based on where it's an offensive, defensive, or kicking play so they're easily identifiable. Once your video is on Hudl you can then import any ODK/tagging information that you did in your EDGE App into Hudl as well. This will save you time uploading video from your camera to your computer and also save you time coding the game's ODK info as well.

Automation

The EDGE software does everything for you. Just use the remote to record/stop each play.

The EDGE Instant Replay system comes fully configured and ready to go right when you receive it. We have made it virtually plug and play with all software and networking configurations already done for you. Just connect your camera to the capture device, then connect the capture device to your computer, connect the EDGE router and antenna to the computer, connect the iPads to the EDGE wireless network, and start filming.

All the hardware is already configured and is plug and play ready when you get it. There are only a few cables to connect, after that you will be ready to start recording your first plays.

Reliable
