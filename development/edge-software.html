Instant Replay Made Easy

How It Works

Connect

existing HD cameras to system.

The EDGE Recorders will connect to your existing press box and end zone cameras with an HDMI cable. All you need is a high definition camcorder with an HDMI out port.

Record

and stop plays with iPad app.

The EGDE iPad app allows one operator to start and stop the recording of multiple cameras at the same time giving you the same number of clips from each camera angle. All your camera operators have to do is point and zoom the cameras.

Replay

plays on iPad in 2-3 seconds.

After a play is captured by the EDGE Recorder, the play will automatically be sent to every iPad connected to the EDGE Network. Once the play is downloaded to your iPad, you will be able to replay that clip over and over.

Upload

video and ODK directly into Hudl.

After the game is finished and you have an internet connection, upload your video clips and ODK from each angle to Hudl. Since EDGE Replay syncs all angles, your intercut will be extremely easy.

Software & App Features

Full Control

Slow motion, fast forward and rewind each play.

The EDGE App allows the user to have full control over the video playback. Using the speed control meter at the top of the app, you can play each video clip in slow motion and fast forward, both in play and paused modes for simple control. The custom controls of the EDGE App allow forward and rewind with ergonomically positioned thumb buttons over the video player or the play can be scrubbed back and forth using the progress bar feature. Additionally, the play number is positioned in the top left-hand corner so you can quickly see exactly which play you're viewing.

Visual

Color coded drives by ODK. Draw on video to quickly show players needed adjustments.

The EDGE App is designed to be very visual in order to help coaches find the plays they want to review fast. Drives are color coded by ODK and all other features were designed with the same intuitive and visual qualities. Draw over the video to visually show your players needed adjustments. You can draw with three colors: blue, red, yellow and can erase the drawings with the undo button. Drawing on the screen is the simplest way to communicate quickly to your players.

Easy & Predictive Play Tagging

Tag ODK, down & distance, hash, YL, play type, net yards and more.

EDGE Replay is the only software that has easy & predictive play tagging. With EDGE Tagging, an assistant coach or student can quickly tag each play with down, distance, hash, YL, play type, play result, net yards and more. EDGE Tagging is also predictive, which means it automatically keeps track of the down, distance, YL, hash, score, time of possession and more. It also predicts the most likely play results for every scenario. The play tagging is so quick and easy that is will become part of your standard workflow which will lead to future EDGE Replay features such as live game stats and opponent tendencies.

Multiple Synced Camera Angles

Sync your press box and end zone camera angles.

Connect as many EDGE Recorders to the EDGE Network as you want. Each box will automatically connect and sync with the others on the network. Press the record button in the EDGE iPad app and all the boxes will record and stop at the same time. Each angle will then download onto every iPad that is connected to the network.

Picture in Picture Angle Switching

See up to two angles on the video player.

Switch between angles by using the picture in picture (PIP) screen in the bottom right corner of the video player. The playback of each angle will stay synced as you switch between angles with the PIP.

Smart Features

Quickly sort clips by tagged information. Save clips to device to show in locker room at halftime.

The EDGE App comes with many built in smart features that save coach's time and allow them to fully use EDGE Instant Replay to find and exploit their opponent's weaknesses. One of those features is the ability to quickly sort through clips based on the tagged information assigned to each play. This allows each coach to tailor the EDGE App to their coaching position and style. Offensive, defensive, and special teams coaches can filter the plays that matter the most to them, which eliminates clutter and gets them viewing the most valuable plays to quickly see what adjustments need to be made. Coaches can also save certain plays to their iPad and bring them into the locker room to show their players during halftime. This can be done without being connected to the wireless network because the plays are saved to the device.

Time Saver

Upload your synced video clips and ODK directly into hudl.

All the video clips that are recorded during a game are saved on the EDGE Recorder & your iPad. The plays can be easily accessed and uploaded to Hudl. You can easily tag each play by ODK, down & distance, hash, YL, play type, net yards, and much more. Each play is is color coordinated based on whether it's an offensive, defensive, or kicking play so they're easily identifiable. Once your video is on Hudl you can then import any ODK/tagging information that you did in your EDGE App into Hudl as well. This will save you time uploading video from your camera to your computer and also save you time coding the game's ODK info as well.

Automation

The EDGE software does everything for you. Just use the your iPad to record/stop each play.

The EDGE Instant Replay system comes fully configured and ready to go right when you receive it. We have made it virtually plug and play with all software and networking configurations already done for you. Just connect your camera to the EDGE Recorder, connect the EDGE router and antenna to the recorder, connect the iPads to the EDGE Network, and start filming.
