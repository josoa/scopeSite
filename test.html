Connect

existing HD cameras to system.

The EDGE Recorders will connect to your existing press box and end zone cameras with an HDMI cable. All you need is a high definition camcorder with an HDMI out port.

Record

and stop plays with iPad app.

The EGDE iPad app allows one operator to start and stop the recording of multiple cameras at the same time giving you the same number of clips from each camera angle. All your camera operators have to do is point and zoom the cameras.

Replay

plays on iPad in 2-3 seconds.

After a play is captured by the EDGE Recorder, the play will automatically be sent to every iPad connected to the EDGE Network. Once the play is downloaded to your iPad, you will be able to replay that clip over and over.
